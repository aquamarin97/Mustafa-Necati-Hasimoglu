### 🚀 Featured Projects

#### [PharmaLyzer SMA](https://github.com/aquamarin97/PharmaLyzer-SMA)
Production-grade desktop application for real-time PCR analysis in SMA carrier screening — deployed in public health screening programs.
Built with Python/PyQt5 — features automated 96-well plate processing, statistical classification (KMeans + L-BFGS-B optimization), regression-based QC, and interactive data visualization at 60+ FPS.

#### [NgDXR Drug Design](https://github.com/aquamarin97/SBDD_NgDXR)
MSc thesis project: Structure-based drug design targeting antibiotic-resistant *N. gonorrhoeae*. Screened ~4.5M compounds via hierarchical Glide docking (HTVS→SP→XP), performed 100 ns MD simulations (AMBER18), and identified 17 novel DXR inhibitors with MM/GBSA validation. International collaboration (Germany × Thailand × Turkey). Funded by TÜBİTAK (Project No. 121N818).

#### Primer Hunter *(in progress)*
End-to-end qPCR target optimization pipeline — conserved region detection, primer/probe design, and off-target screening with automated reporting.

#### *T. annulata* LDH Inhibitor Discovery
Structure-based identification of novel inhibitors targeting lactate dehydrogenase in *Theileria annulata* — a veterinary parasite causing tropical theileriosis. YTU Scientific Research Projects.
